About Me

I am a user experience and user interface designer currently living in San Francisco. Growing up, watching Star Trek with my father inspired me to be interested in the prospects of technology and humanities. After spending extensive amount of time in Taiwan and studying abroad in Japan, it is my belief that the interpretation of people's behavior is contextual. I believe by applying that knowledge as the foundation to design, we can create pleasant and seamless solutions to difficult problems.
